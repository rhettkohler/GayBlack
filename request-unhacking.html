Recovery intake

Request Unhacking

Use this form for defensive help with suspicious access, account recovery, device concerns, phishing, or digital safety planning. Do not send passwords, recovery codes, private keys, or ID documents.

What happens next

Send the essentials. Keep secrets out.

The submission is emailed to the GBMH intake address. The first submission may require the destination email to confirm FormSubmit activation before messages are delivered.

  • Describe what changed and when you noticed it.
  • List affected accounts or devices by platform name only.
  • Keep credentials, access codes, and private documents out of the form.

For emergencies involving immediate physical danger, contact local emergency services first.
